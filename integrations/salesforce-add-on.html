The Salesforce Module enables the HapYak Analytics engine to pass data directly to your Salesforce instance. The module will automatically create Leads, Contacts and Campaign objects, or connect to existing objects. For advanced users, the module can be configured to work with Salesforce Custom Objects.

Requirements

  • You must be an Enterprise Salesforce customer with access to "Integration with Web Service API".

  • You must be an Enterprise HapYak customer and have the Salesforce Add-on added to your account. Contact support@hapyak.com to get that added.
