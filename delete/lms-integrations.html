HapYak integrates easily with your LMS.  You can pass in User ID and related information to track individual users as they interact with the video and personalize the viewing experience for them. You can also send our robust reporting and analytics data to your LMS.

There are a number of other custom functionalities that are enabled through an LMS integration. To get started, email us at info@hapyak.com. In addition, you can always use our APIs to connect your LMS or other external systems.
