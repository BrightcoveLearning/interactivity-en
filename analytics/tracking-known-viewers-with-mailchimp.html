Here's a great trick to pass in known viewer information from an email campaign in MailChimp. When you add a link to the interactive video in MailChimp, add query parameters to the end that reference the MailChimp merge tags. That will automatically identify who is watching the video to Brightcove Interactivity analytics.

Here's an example:

http://corp.hapyak.com/let-your-role-define-your-goal/?hapyak_userId=*|EMAIL|*&hapyak_first=*|FNAME|* 
Note: This is a proof of concept only provided to show the possibilities of the Brightcove Interactivity Platform and should not be taken as an offer to create, edit or maintain custom integration or development.
