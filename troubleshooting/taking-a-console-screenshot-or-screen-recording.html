Occasionally Brightcove Support may as you to open your the console in your browser and take a screenshot or screenrecording of what you see. This helps the Brightcove team quickly reproduce the issue and determine what the root cause of the issue is.

How to open the console

Firefox:  Ctrl+Shift+K (Windows / Linux) or Cmd+Opt+K on (Mac)
Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+J (Windows / Linux) or Cmd+Opt+J (Mac)
Safari: Cmd+Opt+C
Microsoft Edge: F12
