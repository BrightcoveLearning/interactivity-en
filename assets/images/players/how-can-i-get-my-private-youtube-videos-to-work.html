--- title: How can I get my private YouTube videos to work? description: Getting private YouTube videos to work with HapYak. parent: Players ---

{{ page.title }}

{{ page.description }}

1. Log in to your Youtube account

2. Log in to your HapYak account.
3. On the top right, click your account name. Click the orange "edit" icon to edit your account settings.
4. Click the Google "Connect" button. Hit "Accept".
5. On subsequent sessions in HapYak, ensure that you are logged into Google.
6. When you share HapYak videos that use private YouTube videos as the source, the viewer must have access to and be logged in to the Google account that contains the private YouTube video.
