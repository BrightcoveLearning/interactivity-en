Localization (sometimes referred to as NLS - Native Language Support)﻿ is easy with HapYak. Customize the language used on buttons, prompts and labels by using a Localization File. Here is the localization template. Download it. Replace the text inside the quotes with the translation you'd like.

Here are a few examples that have already been created.

Chinese
French
German
Hebrew
Korean
Norwegian
Spanish
Swedish
Turkish

Once you have added the translations, email it to us or upload it to your CMS and Paste the URL to your localization file in the Languageof the Configure panel on a Project page.﻿ We can also automatically apply it to all your projects.  Brightcove Support Email us the file to do so.
