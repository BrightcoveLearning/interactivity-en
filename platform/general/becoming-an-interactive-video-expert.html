Brightcove Interactivity is very easy to get started with. Soon thereafter most folks ask us "how do I become an expert?" Here's the pattern that most expert users follow.

  1. Establish best practices and style guides.
  2. Create a repeatable process using templates and core projects that fit your use case.
  3. Produce video with interactivity in mind.
