Follow these steps to change the video source for a project.

  1. Login to Brightcove Interactivity Studio.
  2. In the left navigation, click Manage.
  3. Click on a project to open it.
  4. At the top right hand corner of the page, click SETTINGS.
  5. Click the current video link next to Video Source.
  6. Edit the Embed Code or URL field.
  7. If the player provider is different and an error is thrown, update the Embed type.
  8. Click NEXT.
