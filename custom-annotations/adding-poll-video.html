The Poll widget is a powerful, versatile tool for data collection. The Poll widget allows you to enter a question or prompt, upload a cover image and create different poll types. A Poll report is available to provide the aggregate data on all of the polls.

Poll types

The Poll widget can be configured for multiple choice, true or false or picture choice.

Multiple Choice

Supports 2 to 4 answer choices.

True or False

Choose between True or False, Yes or No, and Like or Dislike.

Picture Choice

Supports up to four picture choices.

Adding a poll annotation

Follow these steps to add a poll to a project:

  1. Login to Brightcove Interactivity Studio.
  2. In the left navigation, click Manage.
  3. Click on a project to open it.
  4. Click the widget icon in the bottom toolbar.
  5. Click the Poll widget.
  6. Click ADD. The widget is added on top of a sample video.
  8. In the bottom toolbar, click on the image overlay button.
  9. Click Choose File and browse and select an image or click the URL tab and enter the URL to an image file.
    Note: Brightcove recommends using https image URLs. Http images will not load in HapYak Studio, however, they will appear when the video is published.
  10. Click Show.
  11. The image will display on the Preview tab. Click SUBMIT.
  12. Click and drag the overlay to where you want it to appear on the video.
    Note: Make sure the overlay isn't placed at the bottom of the player where it may be covered up by the control bar.
  13. Click the Start Time menu.
  14. Enter a Start time in seconds, as a percentage or in time format. This example sets the start time to the 75% point of the video. Click SUBMIT.
  15. Click the clock icon to set the duration of the overlay. Click SUBMIT.
  16. Click the Link menu.
  17. Enter a URL to direct to when the overlay is clicked.
  18. (Optional) Click the BEHAVIOR tab and have the video Pause on video click. Click SUBMIT.
  19. Click Done.

Publish the video using the Media module and confirm that the overlay appears.

Adding a poll annotation

  1. Select Add IFrame from the annotation menu (the pink icon highlighted above). 
  2. Select Poll from the Widget Library and click Add.
  3. Choose your poll type.
  4. Add a question or prompt, description, and cover image.
  5. Fill out any answer choices and adjust any settings. 
  6. Click Save.

Editing polls

From the main edit screen, users can also edit the settings, positioning, and results page for their polls. 

The Settings menu allows users to customize the primary and secondary colors, adjust the borders, or even add a background music track. 

The Positioning menu allows for positioning the poll on the left, right, or center of the screen. 

The Results Page menu shows results of the poll, along with editable fields for a message and image. 

Reporting

The Poll Widget Summary report can be found on the "Core Reports" section of the HapYak platform. Users can review all polls available across their group with information on widget usage.
