Recently we got a question. "On the quizzes, can it be set so that if the learner fails a quiz they cannot move forward in a video?"

Yes - that is supported out of the box.

When you are configuring a Quiz, 

  1. Click " Edit" on the annotation

  2. Set " Gate:Y"

  3. Under Options / Review Screen, uncheck "Allow viewer to see correct answer(s)"

  4. Under Options / Pass/Fail Actions, click " On Fail"  and then set " Jump to Video Time"to 0 seconds, and finally, check "Reset questions"

A viewer will not be able to proceed in the video until they have correctly answered the question.
