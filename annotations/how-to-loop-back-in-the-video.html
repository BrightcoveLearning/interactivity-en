With a loop back, a section of the video will play in a loop until the viewer clicks on something.

Here's how to add one.

  1. Create a text, image, or link annotation.
  2. Click the Edit menu on the annotation and then click Link.
  3. In the Behavior tab, click the "Actions to take if the viewer doesn't click" dropdown menu, and select Loop Backï»¿.
